Importance of Front Page:
The home page of a website is often the first page that a visitor sees, so it is important for the home page to make a good impression and give a clear idea of what the website is about. A well-designed home page can help to establish the website's brand and credibility, and can also provide a clear navigation structure for users to find the information they are looking for. In addition, the home page is often the page that is most heavily linked to from other websites, so it can play a key role in the website's search engine rankings. Overall, the home page is an important part of a website and plays a crucial role in how users perceive and interact with the website.
